Calico House Cat

Due to genetic determination of coat colors linked to the X chromosome, calicoes are nearly always female.

  • Scientific name:Felis silvestris catus
  • Average lifespan:12 - 15 years
  • Average weight:2 - 16.5 lbs
  • Habitat:Human homes, back alleys

"Calico" refers only to a color pattern on the fur, not to a breed or two-colored eyes on a cat. Among the breeds whose standards allow calico coloration are the Manx, American Shorthair, British Shorthair, Persian, Arabian Mau, Japanese Bobtail, Exotic Shorthair, Siberian, Turkish Van, Turkish Angora and others.
